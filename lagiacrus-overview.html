Species Description

Known as the "Lord of the Seas," Lagiacrus are Leviathans that exist atop the vast aquatic food chain. They generate electricity via their hides to store in their spinal organs, capable of discharging a current strong enough to boil the seawater around them. While they are masters of the water, they are also formidable opponents on land.

Information

Lagiacrus is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds. It is a Leviathan that inflicts Thunderblight and Waterblight status effects. Large Monsters such as Lagiacrus are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. Notable for the glowing shockspikes on its back, hunters are advised to destroy these to limit its electrical capabilities.
