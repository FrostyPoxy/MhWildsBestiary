Species Description

Fearsome male wyverns known as the "King of the Skies." Alongside the Rathian, they stake out a wide territory around their nests, and it is the male's role to watch from above. Armed with powerful poisonous claws and fiery breath, Rathalos use their aerial prowess to descend to invaders

Information

Rathalos is a Large Monster in Monster Hunter Wilds (MHWilds). Rathalos is a Flying Wyvern that inflicts fire and poison status effects. It is the flagship monster of the series. Large Monsters such as Rathalos are hostile and are usually the primary objective of Hunts. They provide valuable Materials when defeated, that allow Hunters to craft gear.
