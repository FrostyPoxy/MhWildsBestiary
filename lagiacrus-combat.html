Lagiacrus Moveset

Thunder Ball

Lagiacrus charges electricity in its mouth and spits a ball of lightning at the hunter. This projectile travels in a straight line and can inflict Thunderblight on contact.

Electric Discharge

Lagiacrus curls its body and releases a massive burst of electricity from the Shockspikes on its back. This area-of-effect attack deals massive damage to anyone standing too close.

Hip Check

A classic Leviathan move. Lagiacrus turns its side towards the hunter and slams its hip and tail sideways. This attack has a deceptively large hitbox.

Body Slam

Lagiacrus raises its upper body and slams it down onto the ground. If charged, this attack may release residual electricity upon impact.

Charging Bite

Lagiacrus lunges forward with its mouth open, attempting to bite the hunter. It may perform this multiple times in succession when enraged.

Tail Whip

Lagiacrus swings its long tail around. Due to the length of the tail, this attack covers a wide arc behind and to the sides of the monster.

Lightning Slide

Lagiacrus slides forward on its belly, surrounded by electricity. This gap-closing move is fast and dangerous if the hunter is caught in the path.
